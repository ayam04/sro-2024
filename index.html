Event Plan

The Timeline

  • Treasure Hunt

    August 17, 2024

    An exciting treasure hunt, promising a day filled with adventure, teamwork, and fun. Participants will embark on a thrilling quest, navigating through various clues and challenges scattered across the sprawling campus. This event is designed to encourage collaboration and problem-solving skills as teams work together to decipher hints and uncover hidden treasures. Along the way, interns will explore different campus landmarks, fostering a sense of camaraderie and discovery. With intriguing puzzles and engaging activities, the treasure hunt promises an unforgettable experience

  • Murder Manor

    August 18, 2024

    Explore a mysterious manor to uncover hidden clues and piece together evidence. The manor holds secrets and potential suspects, challenging you to use your detective skills to identify the murderer. This immersive experience will use critical thinking and teamwork, with a gripping narrative that keeps everyone on the edge of their seats. The event culminates in the exciting revelation of the culprit.

  • NBC Mela

    August 21, 2024

    A vibrant "Mela", featuring a lively blend of cultural performances, food stalls, games, and crafts. This festive celebration offers a perfect opportunity for everyone to relax, socialise, and experience diverse traditions, creating lasting memories and fostering a sense of community.

  • Culinary Workshop

    August 22, 2024

    Food has the incredible power to unite, and there's something truly special about crafting it with your own hands. Everyone will have the unique opportunity to prepare their favourite dishes using the state-of-the-art kitchen facilities at MUJ.

  • Cultural Evening

    August 24, 2024

    An evening filled with mesmerising cultural performances. From soul-stirring music and captivating dance performances to engaging skits and a dazzling fashion show, the program promises a delightful variety of presentations. This immersive showcase is designed to not only entertain but also deepen our understanding and appreciation of the myriad traditions that make India truly unique.

  • Movie Night

    August 25, 2024

    The ultimate cinematic adventure where everyone will gather together for an epic movie-watching experience! As we settle in with our favourite snacks and cozy blankets, we'll embark on a journey through different worlds, genres, and stories. It is the perfect way to escape reality and immerse yourself in the art of storytelling.

  • Prom Night

    August 26, 2024

    Proms transcend mere dances; they symbolise rites of passage, eagerly anticipated with excitement. The evening unfolds with a blend of music, dance, and socialising, marking a memorable and significant experience for all.
